Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and published by Vivek Gaikwad under the brand Rise of Rebels. By using our apps, you agree to the terms described here. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with full transparency.

Data We Collect

Depending on the app you use, we may collect:

  • Device information — model, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data — features used, session duration, crash reports
  • Camera/storage access — only for scanning apps, with your explicit permission
  • Account data — email address if you sign in via Google
  • Analytics — anonymised usage patterns via Firebase Analytics

🔒 We never sell your personal data to any third party. Ever.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected data solely to:

  • Operate and continuously improve our apps
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs quickly
  • Personalise your in-app experience
  • Send important service notifications (not marketing spam)
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Our apps use these trusted third-party services, each governed by their own policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Auth)
  • Google AdMob — for free, ad-supported apps
  • Google Gemini API — powering AI features

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Documents scanned in our apps are processed locally on your device wherever possible and are never uploaded without your explicit consent.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it without delay.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. Continued use of our apps after changes means you accept the revised policy. We encourage you to review it regularly.
